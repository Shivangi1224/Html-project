Taco Trivia!

When did tacos first appeared in United States?

Specifically around 1905 in Los Angeles, California, brought by Mexican workers arriving for railroad construction and mining jobs. (Source: Smithsonian Magazine)

What were tacos known as?

Tacos were often served by street vendors, known as "chili queens," in cities like San Antonio in the early 20th century.
